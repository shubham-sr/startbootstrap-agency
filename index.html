Business

Business Model

The Company follows Multilevel Marketing system for profit distribution. Before expressing in details, we first discuss what the Multilevel Marketing is?

It is clear from the name that

Multi=Many

Level=Level

Marketing=Marketing.

That means those types of Marketing that can be made on more than 1 Level or many Level. The Company has fixed a Level of 11 up to which we will pay remuneration to our Direct Sellers, that means we will give income till 11th Level to our Direct Sellers.

Now, the question is, what is the meaning of Level?

  • When you use or sell a Product/Service, it is called your 1st Level.

  • When the people referred by you use or sell a Product/Service, it is called your 2nd Level.

  • When the people referred by the people that are referred by you use or sell a Product/Service, it is called your 3rd Level.

    • Similarly, the process will continue to 11th Level because the Company give remuneration to 11th Level as mentioned above.

    Another thing is that what is the percentage of the total cost of a Product/Service that will be paid to a Direct Seller for each sell at different Level?

    The Company has fixed the percentages as shown below:-

      On 1st Level: 7%
      2nd Level: 5%
      3rd Level: 5%
      4th Level: 4%
      5th Level: 4%
      6th Level: 3%
      7th Level: 3%
      8th Level: 3%
      9th Level: 2%
      10th Level: 2%
      11th Level: 2%.
